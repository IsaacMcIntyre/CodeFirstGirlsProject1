About


Calderdale

Calderdale takes the name from the dale of the River Calder. Calderdale is the home to 6 towns and several villages. This beautiful part of Yorkshire has lots to offer all year round. Calderdale is home to the Piece Hall which has recently been renovated and now has shops, music festivals and all year round exciting events.


Calderdale is also famous for its walks, canals and cycle routes. Calderdale is a special part of Yorkshire. Few locations offer this blend of beauty and history in such a small place. Our location in Pennine Yorkshire gives us the wonderful rivers, hills, valleys and moorland, but Calderdale is much more than beauty and great views. Calderdale is full of places that need to be there, that have a purpose, not just places to see. These places are part of the soul of Calderdale, places which help to tell Calderdale’s story. And what a story it is. There’s no better way to immerse yourself and discover more about this beautiful area than on foot.
